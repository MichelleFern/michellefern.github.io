What are A.P.I.

API’s expose us as the user to something useful, just as a well brings and delivers water to its user. Developers write programs which consumes the APIs that will transfer into a way that can be read seamlessly and almost instantly. Web APIs are different from those found on your computer, here you communicate through HTML. Before services found it complicated to communicate without the hassle of time consumption. With API’s you can have a request for a certain task, say your Uber has almost arrived to you, it is APIs that will automatically track the driver, pull your information, as well as send notifications straight to the user’s device.
