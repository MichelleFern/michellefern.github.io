GWDA283: Advanced Web Design

Assignment: Group project - Find a client and create a website to their specifications

Team:

Michelle Fern: Web Tech Lead

Edward Crocker: Project Manger

Clarissa Ray: Art Director

Eric Dunn: Usabilty Lead

Challenges: This was my first team project and I discovered how vital communication is, especially when our client contact person missed several important meetings. The client was very specific in her vision and the necessity for good communication was very clear.

We struggled at first to design something we could all be proud to produce, but as a team pulled together to create a site the client absolutely loved. I learned a lot about working both in teams, and also with a client with a very different design aesthetic.

Highlights: The biggest highlight for me was coding the site. I really enjoyed having the design handed to me and figuring out how to make it happen.

Besides coding the site, the challenges were the best part of this assignment. Before this class, I thought that I did not like working in a group. Now I know that, in general, I would actually prefer to work in a group.
