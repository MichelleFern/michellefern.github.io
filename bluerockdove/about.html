I started making my own jewelry 7 years ago because I loved real stones like turquoise, carnelian, and citrine. I found that the quality of stones and workmanship in stores and on Etsy were rather lacking especially since prices were usually well over $100 each. Every time I wore one of my own creations people would ask me where I got it. Each piece I make is a one of a kind. I only purchase and use stones I feel a connection to, and make me happy. I hope my creations make you happy as well
